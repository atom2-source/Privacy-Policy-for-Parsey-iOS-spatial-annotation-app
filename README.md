# Privacy Policy — Parsey

**Effective Date:** 15 April 2025  
**Developer:** Parsey Engineering  
**Contact:** parseyc@gmail.com

---

## Overview

Parsey is a spatial annotation tool for iPhone Pro that operates entirely on-device. Your annotation data, LiDAR scans, project files, and exported datasets never leave your device and are never transmitted to Parsey Engineering or any third party.

---

## Data We Collect

Parsey itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. However, Parsey contains third-party SDKs that collect data as described below.

### Advertising — Google AdMob

Parsey displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data to serve personalised advertising:

- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- Approximate location (derived from IP address)
- App interaction and usage data
- Advertising performance data

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time via **Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising** on your iPhone.

### Subscriptions — StoreKit 2

Parsey Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit 2 framework. Parsey Engineering never has access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. All subscription data is managed by Apple in accordance with [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Crash Diagnostics

Basic crash and performance data may be collected through Apple's standard crash reporting infrastructure if you have opted in to share analytics with developers on your device. Parsey Engineering does not use any third-party crash reporting SDK.

---

## On-Device Data

The following data is processed locally on your device and never transmitted externally:

- LiDAR depth scans and ARKit spatial data
- 3D bounding box annotations and keypoints
- Project files and object class definitions
- Exported YOLO and COCO format datasets

---

## Environment Scanning

Parsey uses your iPhone Pro's LiDAR Scanner and ARKit to scan your physical surroundings for the purpose of placing 3D annotations. This spatial data is used exclusively within the app for annotation purposes and is not stored beyond your active session or exported outside of your explicit export actions.

---

## Children's Privacy

Parsey is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
